    About Our Institution

    Sacred Heart Degree College is committed to excellence in education and providing world-class resources for academic growth and research.

    Our College Legacy

    Established in 1998, Sacred Heart Degree College operates under Article 30(1) of the Indian Constitution and is proudly affiliated with the University of Lucknow.

    Recognized as one of the premier institutions under Lucknow University, we maintain exceptional academic standards while fostering value-based education.

About Sacred Heart College

Sacred Heart Degree College, Sitapur, founded in 1998 is a self financed degree college of minority nature in accordance with the provisions of Article 30(1) of the Constitution of India (Rights of Minorities to establish and administer educational institutions) and affiliated to the University of Lucknow.

Sacred Heart Degree College is one of the leading colleges of University of Lucknow and enjoys a rare social accreditation in recognition of her earnest efforts to impart value based education while upgrading herself constantly to address the ever changing demands of the Education sector.

Library Rules & Regulations

Timings: Library will be open from 8:45 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.

Silence: Strict silence shall be observed in the library.

Issuance: Books will be issued only on library card.

Duration: Books issued for 7 days only.

Late Fees: Overdue charge of Rs. 5/- per day.

Card Loss: Fine of Rs. 15/- for lost library card.

Restrictions: No sub-lending of library books.

Care: Handle books carefully - damage/loss requires replacement or double cost payment.

Markings: No marking, writing, or underlining in books.

No Dues: Clear all dues before examination admit card issuance.
